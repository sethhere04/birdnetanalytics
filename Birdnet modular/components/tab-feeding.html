💧 Water Features

Birds need water for drinking and bathing. Adding water features can significantly increase bird activity.

🛁 Bird Bath
Essential for all species. Keep shallow (1-2 inches) and clean daily.
⛲ Fountain
Moving water attracts more birds. Sound and sparkle increase visibility.
💦 Dripper
Slow drip creates sound that attracts birds from a distance.

🧹 Feeder Maintenance

Proper maintenance keeps birds healthy and coming back.

✅
Daily
Check water levels, remove spoiled seed, observe bird health
🧼
Weekly
Clean feeders with hot soapy water, disinfect bird baths, rake ground under feeders
🔧
Monthly
Deep clean all feeders, check for damage, rotate feeder locations
